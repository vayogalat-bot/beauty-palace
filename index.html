Our story

A salon built on care & craft

The Beauty Palace is a beloved hair and nail salon nestled in the heart of San Pawl il-Baħar. We've built our reputation on one thing: making every client feel truly looked after.

From precision haircuts and gorgeous colour to flawless manicures and French nail art, our talented team — including our much-loved nail specialist Monique — brings expertise and heart to every appointment.

Whether you're a regular or walking in for the first time, you'll find a clean, welcoming space where professionalism meets warmth.
